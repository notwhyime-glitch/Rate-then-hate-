3. Data we collect

Rathe collects and temporarily processes the following data to operate the service:

Microphone & camera: Audio and video streams are transmitted peer-to-peer directly between matched users via WebRTC. We do not record, store, or have access to your audio or video at any point.

Matchmaking data: A temporary anonymous peer ID is generated each session and stored in Firebase Realtime Database to facilitate matching. This ID is deleted from our lobby queue immediately after a match is made.

Ratings: Numerical ratings (1–10) you submit are stored in Firebase under your anonymous session ID linked to that match. Ratings are not tied to any personal identity.

Signalling data: Temporary connection signals are stored briefly in Firebase and automatically discarded.

We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifiable information during normal use.
